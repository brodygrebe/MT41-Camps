Why “41”?

41 is personal and Montana‑proud — your college jersey number, the Montana State Bobcats legacy number, and Montana’s place as the 41st state. MT41 is a mindset: earn it, own it, and bring it back to your team.

  • Mental & physical development
  • Scheme‑free fundamentals every coach approves
  • Competitions spread throughout to build poise under pressure

What Athletes Work On

  • Footwork, leverage, safe tackling form
  • Offense focus: QB/WR/RB/OL mechanics
  • Defense focus: DB/LB/DL reaction & pursuit
  • Mental lessons: goals → accountability → leadership

Sample 3‑Hour Camp Flow

Warm‑Up

Dynamic mobility, activation, speed prep.

Fundamentals

Agility, ball security, blocking fits, non‑contact tackling form.

Competitions

It's a secret, be ready for anything.

Offense Focus

QB drops & release, WR releases & breaks, RB vision & security, OL footwork & hands.

Defense Focus

DB backpedal & break, LB shuffle & angles, DL get‑off & hand usage.

Competitive Situations

1v1 & 2v1 non‑contact tackling fits; 1v1 OL vs DL pass rush.

FAQ

Who can attend?

Athletes entering grades 7–12.

Is there contact?

No live contact. Tackling instruction is non‑contact form and bag work only.

What should athletes bring?

Cleats, water bottle, athletic gear, sunscreen (outdoor), and a great attitude.
